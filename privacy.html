Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Jobsy is built around a simple principle: we want to see as little of your data as possible, and keep nothing we don't need.

What we collect

When you use Jobsy's Free or Pro tier, the text you paste or upload (your resume, a job description, a LinkedIn summary) is sent to an AI provider to generate a result. Specifically:

  • Free tier — your text is sent to Google's Gemini API, processed, and the response is returned to your browser. We do not store this text on our servers.
  • Pro tier — your text is sent directly using your own Anthropic API key, which you provide and which is stored only in your browser's session storage (cleared when you close the tab). We never see or store this key.
  • Job search — your search terms are sent to Adzuna's job listings API through our server, using our credentials, so your search terms pass through our backend but are not logged or stored beyond the request.

What we don't collect

We don't require an account, a name, an email address, or any payment information to use Jobsy. We don't use cookies, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels. We don't have a database that stores your resume, your cover letters, or anything else you generate.

Error monitoring

If something breaks while you're using Jobsy, an error report (via Sentry) may be sent that includes technical details about the failure — but not the content of your resume, job description, or any personal information you've entered.

Third parties

Your text is processed by Google (Gemini API) or Anthropic (Claude API, Pro tier only), and job searches are processed by Adzuna. Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data sent to their APIs. We encourage you to review theirs if you have specific concerns about AI provider data handling.

Your rights

Because we don't store your data, there's generally nothing to request the deletion of. If you have any concern about a specific interaction, contact us and we'll look into it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a way that matters, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
